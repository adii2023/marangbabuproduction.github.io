More About Me

Hello, Santali enthusiasts! My name is Madha Marang, and I am a passionate YouTuber dedicated to sharing Santali culture, music, and traditions through my videos.

As a native Santali speaker and proud member of the Santali community, I believe in the power of digital media to connect people, preserve our heritage, and promote a sense of belonging among Santali individuals worldwide.

On my YouTube channel, you'll find a diverse range of content that celebrates Santali art forms, folklore, festivals, and much more. I strive to create engaging and informative videos that entertain, educate, and foster a deeper appreciation for our rich Santali heritage.

Whether you're interested in Santali music, dance, traditional crafts, or simply want to explore the vibrant Santali culture, my channel is the perfect destination for you. Join me on this incredible journey as we delve into the captivating world of Santali traditions and celebrations.

Don't forget to subscribe to my channel and hit the notification bell to stay updated with the latest Santali videos. Let's come together as a community, share our stories, and keep our Santali culture alive!
